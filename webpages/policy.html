Refund and Return policy

At ABC.co, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality products and a satisfactory shopping experience. We understand that you might need to return or exchange a product, and we have created a simple return policy to accommodate your needs. Please review the following terms and conditions for returns and exchanges:

Return Period:

You may cancel an order at any time before the products are shipped. Products may be returned within 30 days from the date of delivery

Return Method:

To initiate a return, simply contact our customer support team by calling +1 (808) 591-1063 or by email at customerservice@abcstores.com.You may cancel an order at any time before the products are shipped.

Return Restrictions:

Some items may not be eligible for returns or exchanges, such as personalized items, used or worn items, and items marked as FINAL SALE

Product Condition:

Products being returned must be in new and sellable condition, returned in original packaging with tags still attached, and no visible signs of wear or use

Refunds:

Upon receiving your return, we will process your refund within 7 calendar days. The refund will be issued to the payment method you used during checkout

In some cases, you may opt to exchange your purchase for a different product. To do so, complete the Returns and Exchange form available on our website.Be sure to include the Item Description, SKU#, color, and size. Additional payment may be required for an exchange that is at a higher price
